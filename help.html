Getting Started

How do I register for the Tourist Safety System?

To register for the Tourist Safety System:
  1. Visit the registration page on our website or mobile app
  2. Fill in your personal details including name, email, and phone number
  3. Provide your travel itinerary and accommodation details
  4. Add emergency contact information
  5. Verify your email address and phone number
  6. Complete the registration process

Once registered, you'll receive a digital ID that you can use to access all safety features.

What information do I need to provide during registration?

During registration, you'll need to provide:
  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Nationality and passport details (for international tourists)
  • Travel dates and destinations
  • Accommodation details
  • Emergency contact information
  • Health information (optional)

All information is kept secure and is only used for your safety and emergency response.

Safety Features

How does real-time location tracking work?

The real-time location tracking feature uses GPS technology to monitor your location:
  • Enable location services on your device
  • Allow the app to access your location when prompted
  • Your location is updated in real-time (only during your travels)
  • Location data is encrypted and secure
  • You can pause tracking at any time from settings

This feature helps emergency services locate you quickly in case of an emergency.

What is the emergency alert system?

The emergency alert system allows you to quickly notify emergency services:
  • Press the emergency button in the app or on your smart band
  • Your location and emergency details are sent immediately
  • Nearby emergency services are notified
  • Your emergency contacts are alerted
  • You can communicate with emergency responders through the app

This feature should only be used in genuine emergencies.

Emergency Help

What should I do in case of a medical emergency?

In case of a medical emergency:
  1. Press the emergency button in the app or dial 108 for ambulance
  2. Provide your location and nature of emergency
  3. Follow the instructions provided by emergency services
  4. If possible, have your digital ID ready to show responders
  5. Stay on the line until help arrives

The app will automatically share your location with emergency services.

How do I find the nearest safe zone?

To find the nearest safe zone:
  1. Open the app and go to the "Safe Zones" section
  2. Allow the app to access your location
  3. View the map showing nearby safe zones
  4. Click on any safe zone for more details and directions
  5. Use the navigation feature to get turn-by-turn directions

Safe zones include police stations, hospitals, tourist information centers, and emergency shelters.

Troubleshooting

Why is my location not updating?

If your location is not updating:
  • Check if location services are enabled on your device
  • Ensure the app has permission to access your location
  • Make sure you have a stable internet connection
  • Check if GPS is enabled on your device
  • Try restarting the app

If the problem persists, contact our support team for assistance.

What should I do if I can't receive alerts?

If you're not receiving alerts:
  • Check your notification settings in the app
  • Ensure push notifications are enabled for the app
  • Check if your device is in Do Not Disturb mode
  • Verify your internet connection
  • Make sure your app is updated to the latest version

You can also check your alert preferences in the app settings.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, your personal information is secure. We use:
  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security audits
  • Blockchain technology for sensitive data verification
  • Strict access controls and authentication
  • Compliance with data protection regulations

Your data is only shared with emergency services when absolutely necessary.

Can I use the app internationally?

Yes, the app works internationally with some limitations:
  • Emergency services are specific to India
  • You may incur data roaming charges
  • Some features may be limited outside India
  • Make sure to have an international data plan

For international travel, we recommend purchasing a local SIM card for better connectivity.
