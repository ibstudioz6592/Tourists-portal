Emergency Tips

Know Your Location

Always be aware of your surroundings and know your exact location to share with emergency services.

Save Emergency Numbers

Save important emergency numbers in your phone and keep a written copy as backup.

Stay in Groups

Whenever possible, travel in groups and stay in well-lit, populated areas.

Basic First Aid

Learn basic first aid skills to help yourself and others in emergency situations.

Learn Local Language

Learn basic phrases in the local language to ask for help when needed.

Carry ID

Always carry identification and emergency contact information with you.
